The Area

Woodland Park: The City Above the Clouds

Woodland Park sits at 8,500 feet on the shoulder of Pikes Peak — close enough to Colorado Springs for a dinner out (about 25 minutes), far enough to trade traffic for treetops. Hike aspen-lined trails in summer, watch the hillsides turn gold in fall, wake to fresh snow in winter, and stay up for some of the clearest stargazing in Colorado, every season of the year.
