Questions, answered

Where are the tiny homes located?

All six homes are in Woodland Park, Colorado (80863) — the "City Above the Clouds" at roughly 8,500 feet in the Pikes Peak region, about 30 minutes northwest of Colorado Springs.

How do I book direct?

Use the Check Availability bar at the top of this page — pick your dates and group size and send the request. Your request goes straight to the host team, and you’ll hear back in under a minute, any time of day.

Are the fire pits wood-burning?

Our fire pits are gas — a clean flame at the turn of a knob, with no wood, kindling, or smoke to manage. Perfect for Colorado evenings under the stars.

How many guests can each home sleep?

Our homes range from a cozy retreat for 2 up to homes that sleep 6. Use the guest filter above the grid to see homes that fit your group.

What is there to do nearby?

You’re in the heart of the Pikes Peak region: Pikes Peak (14,115 ft) and its summit highway, Garden of the Gods, Mueller State Park, Cripple Creek, and the shops and restaurants of downtown Woodland Park are all close by.

What if I have questions during my stay?

Message us any time — our host team provides 24/7 concierge-level support and typically replies in under a minute.
